\subsection{Energy Calculation}
 
% Explain the Sobel-based energy function. Reference the formula below.
 
The energy of pixel at row $i$ and column $j$ is computed using the Sobel operator:
 
\begin{align}
    G_x &= -s(i-1,j-1) - 2s(i,j-1) - s(i+1,j-1)
           + s(i-1,j+1) + 2s(i,j+1) + s(i+1,j+1) \\
    G_y &= +s(i-1,j-1) + 2s(i-1,j) + s(i-1,j+1)
           - s(i+1,j-1) - 2s(i+1,j) - s(i+1,j+1) \\
    E(i,j) &= \sqrt{G_x^2 + G_y^2}
\end{align}
